About Our Charity

We wish to bring back relief & joy into lives of those afflicted with Neurological maladies. We started our journey in 1997. A major part of our initial work was done when our Founder was closely associated with KEM Hospital, Mumbai & the distinguished team of Neurologists & Neurosurgeons there. Through his efforts, collaborations were built with Sir Ratan Tata Trust, Sir Dorabji Tata Trust, Lioness Club of Breach Candy, Hargobind Foundation & Dr. A.V. Baliga Trust who donated various sophisticated equipment. This led to the formation of the largest Epilepsy Surgery Centre in Maharashtra. CT guided Stereotaxy (an advanced minimally invasive brain surgery technique) was introduced for the first time in Mumbai for public hospital patients. A Microneurosurgery laboratory was initiated and state of the art Deep Brain Stimulation (Brain Pacemaker for treating Parkinsons patients) was done in a public hospital for the first time in India. Through the Pediatric Neurosurgery Section & aegis of Mr. Vincent, the concept of a Child Friendly ward was introduced in Municipality Hospitals.
